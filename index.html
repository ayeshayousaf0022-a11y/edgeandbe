    Answered

    Frequently asked.

    Where are your bags made?

    In a small family atelier in Florence, Italy. Each bag is completed by a single artisan from cut to finish.

    What leather do you use?

    Full-grain, vegetable-tanned Italian leather sourced from certified Tuscan tanneries.

    Do you ship internationally?

    Yes — UK & EU shipping is free over £400. We ship worldwide with fully tracked, insured couriers.

    What is your return policy?

    Unused bags may be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Custom orders are final sale.

    Do you offer repairs?

    Every Edge & Beyond bag comes with a lifetime repair promise. Get in touch and we'll take care of it.
